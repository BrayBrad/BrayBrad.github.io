If you're in the mood for a something sweet, you'll be spoilt for choice ...

Not content with offering just classic bakery cakes (although you can't go wrong with a Loaf jam doughnut!!), Loaf's baking team has always been keen to adopt new techniques and cake ideas from London and around Europe.

From Swedish cardamon buns and cruffins to vegan chocolate and raspberry mini cakes something new is always being tried and tasted (its a tough job ....!)

The team has recently welcomed a new pastry chef - his award winning macarons , vegan and vegetarian tarts and mini palmiers are gathering a following already ....
