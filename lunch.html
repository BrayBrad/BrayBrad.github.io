Although we believe that a great sandwich starts with great bread, we also make sure that whats inside is great too!
We've selected sandwich ingredients from our Italian suppliers to complement those from closer to home, with quality and taste always the priority.

Our sandwiches and rolls are made every morning (for that day only) using our freshly baked handcrafted bread.
Our menu is ever developing but here are some sandwich options you can expect to find ....

Italian mozzarella with pesto
Wiltshire ham with or without added Italian provolone cheese
New York deli with pastrami, dill gherkins, cheese and a touch of mustard
Roasted chicken with either mayonnaise
Coronation chicken
Caesar chicken & bacon
Bacon, lettuce & tomato
... or keeping it simple - mature Cheddar with a dollop of Branston!

Not forgetting our generously filled sourdough toasties and fresh rosemary focaccia.
From the hot cabinet we offer traditional Cornish pasties, pies as well as the all time favourite, sausage rolls.
